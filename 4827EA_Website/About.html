Our club activities

Tree planting activity

Tree planting activity involves the process of planting trees, typically in designated areas such as parks, public spaces, and forests. It is often carried out as a conservation effort to promote reforestation, mitigate climate change by absorbing carbon dioxide, and improve air quality. Tree planting activity is a crucial step towards achieving a sustainable future, and it also provides numerous benefits to the ecosystem and the community.

Beach recycling activity

Beach recycling activity is a community-based initiative aimed at cleaning up beaches and promoting environmental protection. The activity involves collecting and sorting waste materials from the beach and separating recyclable items such as plastic, glass, and metal from non-recyclable waste. The collected materials are then sent for recycling, reducing the amount of waste that ends up in landfills or pollutes the ocean.
